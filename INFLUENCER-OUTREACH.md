## Partnership Structure

### What We Offer
- **Equity stake** in PokerTime (or parent company)
- **Revenue share** on premium subscriptions from their referral traffic
- **Lifetime premium access** for them + their inner circle
- **Co-branding opportunity** — "PokerTime x [Name]" module or level
- **Early access** — they get to shape the product and give feedback

### Absolute Maximum Equity Per Influencer

| Tier | Followers | Max Equity | Why |
|------|-----------|------------|-----|
| Micro (<50K) | Under 50K | 0.5% | Small but engaged audience, cheap to acquire |
| Mid (50K-250K) | 50-250K | 1% | Real reach, still affordable |
| Large (250K-1M) | 250K-1M | 2% | Significant audience, costs real equity |
| Whale (1M+) | 1M+ | 3% | Only for strategic long-term partners |

**Total equity pool for influencers: cap at 10%** across all partners. Don't give away the company.

### Vesting Terms
- **25% upfront** (when deal is signed)
- **25% at 6 months** (if they've promoted at least 3 times)
- **25% at 12 months** (if still active)
- **25% at 18 months** (if still active + hitting metrics)
- **Cliff:** If they ghost within 90 days, equity reverts
