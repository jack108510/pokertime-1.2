## AGREEMENT

### 1. Equity Grant
Company grants Partner **[X]%** equity in PokerTime, subject to the following vesting schedule:
- **25%** vests immediately upon signing
- **Remaining 75%** vests monthly over 18 months (1/18th per month)
- Vesting begins on the date of signing

### 2. Partner Obligations
Partner agrees to:
1. **Create a minimum of 1 post, story, or video** per month featuring PokerTime for 6 months
2. **Provide honest product feedback** — at least one written review or 30-minute call within 30 days
3. **Allow Company to use Partner's name and likeness** as a promotional partner ("Backed by [Name]")
4. **Share their unique referral link** at least once on their primary platform

### 3. Company Obligations
Company agrees to provide Partner with:
1. **Lifetime Pro access** to PokerTime (all current and future features)
2. **5 additional Pro accounts** for Partner's personal use (friends, family, team)
3. **Co-branded module** — a custom learning module featuring Partner's name and content preferences
4. **20% referral commission** on all Pro subscriptions generated through Partner's unique referral link, paid monthly
5. **Early access** to new features and content before public release

### 4. Exclusivity
This agreement is **non-exclusive**. Partner may promote other products and services. Company may partner with other influencers.

### 5. Intellectual Property
- Partner retains all rights to their existing content
- Company retains all rights to PokerTime
- Content created specifically for this partnership (co-branded module) is jointly owned
- Partner grants Company license to use their name, image, and quotes in PokerTime marketing

### 6. Term
- **Initial term:** 18 months (matching vesting schedule)
- **Auto-renewal:** Monthly after initial term unless either party provides 30 days written notice
- **Referral commissions continue** as long as referred subscribers remain active

### 7. Termination
Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice. Upon termination:
- Partner keeps all vested equity
- Unvested equity is forfeited
- Referral commissions stop for new sign-ups but continue for existing subscribers for 90 days
- Company removes Partner's name from promotional materials within 30 days

### 8. Confidentiality
Both parties agree not to disclose the specific terms of this agreement (equity percentage, commission rate) publicly without mutual consent.

### 9. Governing Law
This agreement is governed by the laws of Alberta, Canada.
